Mr. Van Recognized as FCPS 2020 Outstanding School-based Operational Employee

Congratulations to our building supervisor Long Van who was recognized as the FCPS 2020 Outstanding School-based Operational Employee! Mr. Van came to Camelot as a Custodian II and soon became Building Supervisor due to his hard work and dedication. Mr. Van takes pride in maintaining a clean and safe school environment which makes our school shine. He has a strong work ethic and can be counted on to get the job done. He always goes above and beyond the task at hand. He works well with his custodial staff, and they respect him for his organizational skills and work experience. Mr. Van is very kind cares deeply about the staff and the students at Camelot. He enjoys assisting them in whatever way he can, and he is always positive!
